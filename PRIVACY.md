# Privacy Policy for Wingman

**Last Updated: December 2025**

Wingman ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Wingman browser extension.

## Data Collection and Usage

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

- **Local Storage**: We use your browser's local storage (Chrome Storage API) to save your recent search history for your convenience. This data remains on your device and is never sent to us or any third parties.
- **Flight Search**: When you perform a search, Wingman constructs URLs for third-party flight aggregators (e.g., Skyscanner, Google Flights) based on your input. When you click "Search", these URLs are opened in new tabs. We do not track your activity on these third-party websites.

## Third-Party Services

Wingman redirects you to third-party websites. These websites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their data practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any site you visit.
